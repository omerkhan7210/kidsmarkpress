Our Story

We believe every author deserves a first chance — and every mentor deserves to be remembered.

KidsMark Press was born from a simple truth: the publishing world is harder to enter than it should be. Too many talented voices go unheard — not because their stories aren't worth telling, but because no one handed them the door.

We exist to hand them the door. And to celebrate the people who told them to knock on it in the first place.

Our Mentor Wall isn't a feature. It's our foundation. It's the public record of every teacher who stayed late, every librarian who slipped a book into the right hands, every parent who said "keep going" when everything else said stop.

Author Spotlight

Middle Grade Texas Debut Author

Maria Elena Reyes

Maria Elena grew up in San Antonio, the daughter of two teachers who believed that books were not extras — they were necessities. In fifth grade, her teacher Mrs. Delgado quietly placed a blank journal on her desk with a note that read: "You have something worth saying."

Twenty years later, still carrying that journal, she finished her debut novel. She is now a former school librarian, a first-generation college graduate, and a mentor herself — working with emerging writers through the San Antonio Literacy Council.

How The Mentor Wall Works

From your memory to a public tribute in four steps.

Every approved tribute gets its own permanent URL — yours to share with the mentor, their family, or the world.

1

Write the tribute

Tell us who your mentor is, where they're from, and what they did. Include a photo if you have one.

2

Submit for review

Our team reviews every submission before it goes live. Usually within 48 hours.

3

Tribute goes public

Once approved, your mentor gets their own page — searchable, shareable, permanent.

4

Share the link

Send it to your mentor, post it on social media, or just know you said thank you the right way.
